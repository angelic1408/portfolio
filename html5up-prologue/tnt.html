Tata n Taro Adventure

Thesis Project

Showcased in Advance of Computer Entertainment 2015, Iskandar, Malaysia
Awards: Bronze Awards for Poster Presentation
Publication can be read here


Tata n Taro is my thesis for my master degree at Graduate School of Media Design, Keio University, Japan. As a student, we learnt how to be a media innovator by always applying the design thinking method in our creation - that is, to create human-centered design, rather than technology-centered design.

Idea

The idea is to create a game that allow children to collaborate with other children in different places using the media: Xbox Kinect. The reason to use kinect is described in my publications. The game is targeted to kindergarten children aged from 3 to 6 years old.
